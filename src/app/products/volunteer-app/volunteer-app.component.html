Volunteer App

Community-focused case study for improving volunteer coordination through practical workflow design and clear digital structure.

Product Focus

  • Design a practical digital home where volunteers can quickly find what they need and stay engaged.
  • Reduce coordination overhead by improving visibility, communication flow, and day-to-day task clarity.
  • Support sustainable team growth with lightweight systems that are easy to maintain and iterate.

What Was Improved

  • Clearer navigation and page structure for volunteer actions and information discovery.
  • More consistent workflow pathways so volunteers can move from intent to action with less friction.
  • Better foundation for continuous improvement through practical, feedback-led iteration.

Delivery Highlights

  • Balanced user needs with implementation practicality to keep progress steady and measurable.
  • Used iterative delivery to validate assumptions early and improve confidence before expanding scope.
  • Documented decisions to preserve shared knowledge and support future contributors.
